🚀 PetAlertPro Features

PetAlertPro is designed to help pet owners stay informed about pet food recalls in real time. We source alerts from **official government agencies** to ensure accuracy and reliability.

📢 How Do Alerts Work?

We monitor official **FDA recall announcements** and **RSS feeds** for any pet food-related recalls. Once detected, our system filters out irrelevant recalls (such as livestock feed) and sends you an alert.

Key Features:

  • 🚀 **Real-time monitoring** of pet food recalls.
  • 🔎 **Accurate filtering** to ensure you get only relevant alerts.
  • 📩 **Email alerts** delivered directly to your inbox.

🔍 How Brand Tracking Works

When subscribing, you can **track specific pet food brands** to receive personalized alerts. Here’s how it works:

  • 📌 If you **enter a brand name but don’t check ‘Send alerts only for my tracked brands’**, you will receive all pet food alerts, but the email subject will highlight your tracked brand.
  • ✅ If you **enter a brand and check ‘Send alerts only for my tracked brands’**, you will receive **alerts only for your selected brands**, even if the recall includes **non-pet products**.

⚠️ Limitations

We strive to provide accurate and timely alerts, but there are some limitations:

  • ⏳ **Delays in official sources** – Sometimes, RSS feeds may update with a slight delay.
  • 🛑 **Some brands may not be covered** – If a recall is announced on a source we do not track, it may not appear.
