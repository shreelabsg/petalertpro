🚀 Stay Informed, Protect Your Pet

PetAlertPro is designed to help pet owners receive **timely updates** about pet food recalls. We track announcements from **trusted sources like the FDA** to ensure you never miss an important alert.

📢 How It Works

Our system continuously checks for recall notices. If a recall matches your preferences, you’ll receive an email alert.

  • 🚀 **Continuous Monitoring** – We scan for recalls regularly.
  • 🔎 **Smart Filtering** – Avoids irrelevant alerts.
  • 📩 **Email Notifications** – Delivered directly to your inbox.

🔍 Brand Tracking Explained

You can **track specific pet food brands** and customize the alerts you receive.

📌 Option 1: Enter a brand **without checking** "Send alerts only for my tracked brands." ➝ You’ll receive **all pet food recalls**, but emails will highlight your brand.

✅ Option 2: Enter a brand **and check** "Send alerts only for my tracked brands." ➝ You’ll receive **alerts only if your tracked brand is involved**, even if it’s for a non-pet product.

While we strive to provide accurate and timely alerts, there are some important considerations:

  • ⏳ **Official sources may have delays** – Recalls are published at different times.
