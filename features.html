🚀 PetAlertPro Features

PetAlertPro helps pet owners stay informed about pet food recalls in real-time. We track recalls from **official government agencies** and notify you instantly.

📢 How Do Alerts Work?

We monitor **FDA recall announcements** and **RSS feeds** for pet food recalls. Once detected, our system filters out irrelevant recalls and sends you an alert.

  • 🚀 **Real-time monitoring** of pet food recalls.
  • 🔎 **Accurate filtering** – get only relevant alerts.
  • 📩 **Email notifications** straight to your inbox.

🔍 How Brand Tracking Works

You can **track specific pet food brands** to receive personalized alerts:

  • 📌 **Option 1:** Enter a brand name but do not check ‘Send alerts only for my tracked brands’ → **You get all pet food recalls**, with a personalized subject line highlighting your brand.
  • ✅ **Option 2:** Enter a brand and check ‘Send alerts only for my tracked brands’ → **You receive alerts only for your selected brands**, even if the recall includes **non-pet products**.
