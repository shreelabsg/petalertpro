PetAlertPro Features

🚀 What PetAlertPro Offers

PetAlertPro helps pet owners stay informed about **pet food recalls** based on announcements from trusted sources such as the **FDA**. We ensure that you receive timely updates and accurate alerts.

📢 How Do Alerts Work?

Our system monitors **official recall sources** and filters relevant pet food recalls. If an issue is detected, an email is sent directly to subscribers.

Key Features:

  • 🚀 **Continuous monitoring** of pet food recall announcements.
  • 🔎 **Advanced filtering** to avoid irrelevant alerts.
  • 📩 **Email notifications** sent directly to your inbox.

🔍 How Brand Tracking Works

Subscribers can choose to **track specific pet food brands** for personalized recall notifications. Here's how the tracking system works:

  • 📌 If you **enter a brand name but do not check ‘Send alerts only for my tracked brands’**, you will receive all pet food recall alerts, but emails will highlight your tracked brand.
  • ✅ If you **enter a brand and check ‘Send alerts only for my tracked brands’**, you will receive **alerts only for your selected brands**, even if the recall includes non-pet products.

Example Email Subject:

"🚨 Recall Alert: Issue Detected with Your Tracked Brand XYZ Pet Food"

  • ⏳ **Delay in official updates** – Some recall sources may take time to update.
